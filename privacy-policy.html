Overview

Elite Empire Notary LLC serves customers in Catawba County and surrounding areas. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, protected, and shared when you visit this website, request an appointment, make a payment, or contact us about notary services.

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, service request details, appointment location, preferred appointment time, payment-related information, and any message or document details you choose to provide. Customer information is used to respond to inquiries, schedule and confirm appointments, provide notary services, send appointment reminders and follow-ups, process payments, maintain business records, improve the website, and comply with applicable North Carolina notary requirements.

How Information Is Protected

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect customer information. No website, email system, payment service, or online transmission is completely secure, so customers should use care when sending personal or document-related details electronically.

Information Is Never Sold

Elite Empire Notary LLC does not sell customer personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to provide requested services, process payments, operate business tools, comply with law, protect rights and safety, or with your consent.
