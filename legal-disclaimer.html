If you have questions about your legal rights, legal responsibilities, document language, document type, whether a document is legally valid, or how to complete a document, please contact a licensed attorney before your notary appointment.

Notary Role

A notary may verify identity, witness or acknowledge signatures as allowed by North Carolina law, administer oaths or affirmations, complete notarial certificates, and record required notary information. A notary does not decide whether a document is legally correct or appropriate for your situation.

Customer Responsibility

Customers are responsible for bringing the correct document, understanding the document, confirming signing requirements, arranging any required witnesses, and asking the receiving agency, lender, title company, court, or attorney what is needed.
